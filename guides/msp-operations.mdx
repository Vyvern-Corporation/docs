---
title: 'MSP operations'
description: 'Manage organizations and staff as a managed service provider.'
---

Use this guide if you operate as an MSP account.

## Register and log in

Create an MSP account with `POST /api/msp/register`, then log in with `POST /api/msp/login`.

## Manage organizations

Use these endpoints to manage organizations:

- `GET /api/msp/organizations` to list managed orgs.
- `POST /api/msp/organizations` to create a new org.
- `POST /api/msp/organizations/:orgId/switch` to switch active context.

## Take over an admin-owned org

Use these endpoints to move an admin-owned org into MSP management:

- `POST /api/msp/organizations/search` to look up orgs by admin email.
- `POST /api/msp/organizations/:orgId/takeover` with `adminEmail` and `transferCode`.

## MSP profile and logo

Update profile fields with `PUT /api/msp/profile`.

Upload a logo with `POST /api/msp/logo` using `multipart/form-data` and a `logo` file.

## MSP employees

Use these endpoints to manage MSP staff accounts:

- `GET /api/msp/employees`
- `POST /api/msp/employees`
- `PUT /api/msp/employees/:employeeId`
- `DELETE /api/msp/employees/:employeeId`
